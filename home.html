What is XtreamChaser

Get immersed in a fight where you are the one who chooses your specialty by creating bond with your character's skills.
XtreamChaser is a competitive MMO fighting game. The game matches two players to become one-to-one opponent that will also be allowed to pick their own character.

Different character. Different Personality

Enjoy the story of each character that reflects character's skills and statistics. Each character is with their own pros and cons that will always affect gameplay among players.

Master Your Favorite Character's Skills

Characters have their own ability and special moves or secret techniques. Whatever you might call it. Surprise your opponent from amazing moves and split second decision strategies.

Glorious Finishes

Take your opponent's life with pride by showing your knowledge of cool finishing moves. Each character has numerous finishing blows that can be the color of your play style.
